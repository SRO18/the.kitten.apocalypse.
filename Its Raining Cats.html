Its Raining Cats!

How to Survive The Kitten Apocolypse

They have lived in our homes, slept in our beds, and stolen our hearts. Now, they will seal our world. In this guide we will teach you everything you need to know about defending yourself and your loved ones against these furry lovable invaders.

Why Cats?

Cats are one of the most sinister races of creatures ever created. While seemingly lovable and cuddly their true colors show in their aggression towards humans. Daily attacks disguised as playfulness show their real intentions towards us humans. They wish to attack us and dominate but we are much larger and we keep them in check with fear.

Their Plan

Overwhelm the human population with numbers and attack. Forcing us into a war where sheer numbers will win ownership of the planet. They will force us into concentration camps and enslave us while slowly forcing the human race into extinction.

How Do We Prevent This

  1. Control Their Numbers
    • Keep your cats nutured or spayed so they cannot reproduce as quickly
  2. An unfit cat cannot pose a threat, keep your cat well fed
  3. Minimize their aggressive nature with less "play time" and more cuddle time
  4. Dont let your cat control you. Dont give into unecessary demands for extra food or treats, this is their way of enslaving us to do their bidding

Types Of Cats To Beware

  • The Cuddler
    • Seemingly Sweet but truly one of the most horrifying and deadly types of cats
    • Watch for unwarrented purring or possible ulterior motives for cuddles
  • The Skittish One
    • These little terrors are the stuff of nightmares, making you believe they are docile fear driven creatures when they really just wait till youre not looking to claw off your skin and urinate on everything you love.
  • The Pairs
    • Cats in pairs are twice as evil. They will plot against you and hour house hold to attack and make you do their bidding with twice the cuteness and twice the "love"

Stay Prepared

These homely creatures are a threat to our world as we know it. Stay aware of your home and neighborhood cats. If you can recognize the signs, you can help prevent their evil plans from coming to realization. Everyone must do their part if we are to keep these sweet enemies at bay.
